About Me

Hello! I am Gagan, a dedicated PhD Scholar and Climate Science Researcher in Department of Environmental Sciences and Engineering,(IIT(ISM)), Dhanbad, India. My work primarily focuses on understanding the impact of Arctic amplification over the tropics and mid-latitude circulation and its implications for extreme weather events.

I have completed my Masters in Atmospheric Sciences from NIT, Rourkela, and am currently pursuing my PhD at IIT(ISM), Dhanbad.

My goal is to contribute to a deeper understanding of how Arctic amplification influences mid-latitude circulation, leading to extreme weather events in the subtropics.
